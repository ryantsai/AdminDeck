# dashboard.backgroundFit.center

- **English value**: `Center`
- **Namespace**: `app`
- **File/component**: `src/dashboard/edit/BackgroundPopover.tsx`
- **UI role**: `label`
- **User flow**: A `<select>` option label under the "Fit" control in the BackgroundPopover's "Image" mode. "Center" places the image at its natural size in the middle of the canvas without scaling or repeating.
- **Tone**: concise/neutral, single-word option
- **Placeholders**: none
- **Domain notes**: Equivalent to CSS `background-position: center` with no resize or repeat. The image may be cropped (if larger than the canvas) or surrounded by empty space (if smaller).
